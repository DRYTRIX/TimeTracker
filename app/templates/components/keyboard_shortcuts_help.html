Pro Tips

  • • Press Ctrl K to open the command palette and search for any action
  • • Use sequence shortcuts like g d for quick navigation (type 'g' then 'd')
  • • Most forms can be submitted with Ctrl Enter
  • • Press Esc to close any modal or dropdown
